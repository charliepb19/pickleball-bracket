How to Play

Two modes, one goal — win the championship.

🤝 2v2 Tournament

  • Choose your player — you're randomly paired with a teammate
  • 16 teams seeded by combined rating, single-elimination
  • Best-of-2 sets to 11 (win by 2), tiebreak if 1-1

🌍 World Cup Mode (1v1)

  • 32 players split into 8 groups of 4 — you play all 3 opponents
  • Points: each set you win counts — 2-0 win earns 2pts, 2-1 earns 2pts, 1-2 earns 1pt
  • Tiebreaker: sets won → points for → fewest points against
  • Top 2 from each group advance to the Round of 16
  • Qualifiers seeded 1–16 by group-stage stats into a knockout bracket

Awards

  • Tournament MVP — most points scored across all matches
  • Finals MVP — best individual performance in the championship
